About the Conference

Internal Quality Assurance Cell & Department of Information Technology, SVKM’s Institute of Technology, Dhule, Maharashtra, India is organizing a two-day multidisciplinary international conference on “1st International Conference on Advances In Information Technology (ICAIT 2025)” during August 01–02, 2025.

The conference seeks significant contributions in all major multidisciplinary fields, both theoretical and practical.

The aim of this conference is to provide an excellent international forum for sharing knowledge, exploring recent trends, and engaging in cutting-edge developments in the field.
All accepted and presented papers will be indexed in scopus
